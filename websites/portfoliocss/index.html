Hatsune Miku

CV01

Vocal Synthsizer of Crypton Future Media, Inc., Miku can be anyone that you want her to be.

Initially released as "An Android Diva in the near-future world where songs are lost". This virtual idol has appeared in numberous producers' work.

Notable Facts

  • Voiced by Saki Fujita
  • V4Standard illustrated by iXima
  • Items associated with this character are Spring Onions and Leeks
  • Color associated with this character is teal blue.
  • Crypton Future Media does not approve of the usage of Miku in politics
